# Thursday-Friday-Monday Pattern

Source: Tom Hougaard via time-price-research-astrofin.blogspot.com

## Pattern Definition

Predicts Monday's price action based on previous Thursday and Friday relationship.

**Core Rule:** When Thursday's high exceeds Friday's high â†’ Monday trades lower

---

## Pattern Rules

1. Switch to daily timeframe
2. Confirm Friday's high is **lower than Thursday's high**
3. Mark the low of Friday's candle
4. Move to smaller timeframe for entry
5. Wait for price to reach bearish fair value gap
6. Enter short position
7. Target: Friday's low hit on Monday
8. Monitor regular NY trading session on Monday

## Key Observation

In a 52-week analysis: **20 out of 21 occurrences**, the Dow traded lower on Monday when Thursday's high exceeded Friday's high.

---

## Usage Notes

- Best edge on Nasdaq and Russell 2000
- S&P 500 and DJIA show no statistical edge
- Use as confluence, not standalone strategy
