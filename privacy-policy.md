# Privacy Policy for Trading Agenda

**Effective Date:** January 16, 2026

## Overview
Trading Agenda ("the App") is a mobile journaling application for traders. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is handled.

## Data Collection and Storage

### Local Storage Only
- **All data is stored locally** on your device using SQLite database
- **No cloud synchronization** or remote backup
- **No account creation** required
- Data includes: trade journals, alerts, rules, and session settings

### No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking
- **No analytics services** (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- **No user tracking** or behavioral monitoring
- **No advertising networks**
- **No data sharing** with third parties for marketing purposes

## Permissions

### Camera Permission
- **Purpose:** Allows you to capture screenshots of your trades for journal entries
- **Scope:** Photos are stored locally on your device only
- **Not uploaded** to any server or cloud service

### Photo Library Permission
- **Purpose:** Allows you to attach existing photos/screenshots to your journal entries
- **Scope:** Photos remain on your device only
- **Not uploaded** to any server or cloud service

### Notifications Permission
- **Purpose:** Deliver scheduled alerts for trading sessions and killzones
- **Scope:** All notifications are scheduled locally on your device
- **No remote notifications** or push notification services used

### Storage Permission
- **Purpose:** Store your trade journal data in SQLite database
- **Scope:** Data stored locally in app's private storage directory
- **Accessible only** by the Trading Agenda app

## Crash Reporting
- The App uses **Sentry** for crash reporting to improve app stability
- Crash reports include:
  - Error messages and stack traces
  - Device type and OS version
  - App version
- **No personal data** or trade information is included in crash reports
- You can opt out by not using the app

## Data Retention
- **User-controlled:** All data remains on your device until you delete it
- **Uninstallation:** All data is deleted when you uninstall the app
- **No backups:** We do not maintain any backups of your data

## Data Security
- Data is stored in your device's protected app storage
- Standard SQLite database security applies
- No data transmitted over the internet (except crash reports)

## Children's Privacy
This App is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be reflected in the app with a new "Effective Date."

## Contact
For privacy-related questions or concerns, please create an issue at:
https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/trading-agenda/issues

## Your Rights
Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have full control over your data
- You can delete all data by clearing app storage or uninstalling
- You can export your data through standard Android backup mechanisms (if enabled)

---

**Summary:** Trading Agenda stores everything locally on your device. We don't collect, share, or upload your trading data anywhere. Camera and photo permissions are only for attaching images to your journal entries, and notifications are scheduled locally.
